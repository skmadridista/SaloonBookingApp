About The App

"This saloon booking application is a game-changer for both salon owners and customers alike. The user-friendly interface makes it easy for customers to browse and book appointments at their favorite salons, while salon owners can manage their business more efficiently with the streamlined booking process. The integration of the Knox package for user authentication adds an extra layer of security, ensuring that only authorized users have access to sensitive information. The application's ability to display all the services in an html table makes it easy for customers to view the different services offered by the salon. The application is a must-have for anyone looking to streamline their salon booking process and improve the overall customer experience."
